Class I - II

Float Trips

River water that flows over minimal obstructions waves that make fun splashes. This trip is so relaxing to enjoy time with family with children to make fun memories.

  • Low risk
  • Small waves
  • Easy navigation

Class III

Beginner Level

Navigate easily in a wet and fun trip and you only require some technical skills. This trip is exciting for teenagers with some obstacles to make it challenging.

  • Waves can reach 3 feet high
  • Well-trained guide needed
Book Now!

Class IV

Intermediate Rafters

Big drops of whitewater with obstacles for the unavoidable waves and intermediate rafters require more skills to navigate with precise handling of the boat because those rivers are powerful but still predictable.

  • Higher risk of capsizing
  • Often require complex maneuvering
Book Now!

Class V

Advanced Rafters

Violent rapids are only for advanced rafters who want rapids with major obstacles. The river routes are extremely demanding and require a piece of precise equipment and an extensive experience for the risk.

  • Many obstacles
  • Experts only with experienced guides
Book Now!
